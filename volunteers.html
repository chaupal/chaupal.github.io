How to contribute

We welcome all contributions, if you have formerly been involved in P2P or JXTA, or if you are completely new to its ideas. If you are fluent in our development languages, or if documentation/website design are your areas of expertise - we need you!

We do ask that you agree to the terms in our Contributor Agreement [PDF] before we can accept any pull requests from you.

To join the project, you will need an account on Java.net, and use it to join at https://java.net/projects/jxta

To contribute, you'll need an account on GitHub :)

Or, to just join the dev mailing list, send an email to sympa@jxta.java.net with a subject of subscribe dev

We also have an announce mailing list - send email to sympa@jxta.java.net with a subject of subscribe announce
