Licensing

Why two separate source repositories and licenses?

JXSE is under its own license which cannot be changed. Modifications to it must be under that license. Any new code does not have to be under that license, and to keep things separate, should be committed to the JP2P repository, rather than JXSE. JP2P is under the Apache License, version 2.0.
